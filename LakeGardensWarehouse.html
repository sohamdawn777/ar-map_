The Underground Beat

Tucked away near the hip Lake Gardens neighborhood lies a warehouse that has become an underground temple for Kolkata’s urban artists and hip-hop culture. Its exterior walls are alive with graffiti, while inside, rhythmic beats and murals coexist in an immersive artistic ecosystem. Here, murals reflect global urban art trends—vivid abstract forms, stenciled messages of empowerment, and politically charged iconography. Historically, warehouses like this were industrial relics, but they’ve been repurposed as canvases for expression. The graffiti tells a story of transformation: abandoned spaces turned into creative hubs, and silent walls given voice. Visitors feel the raw energy of youth culture, as the murals echo tales of struggle, aspiration, and the rebellious heart of the city.
