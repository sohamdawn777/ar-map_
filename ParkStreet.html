A Nostalgic Canvas

Park Street has long been Kolkata’s heartbeat of nightlife and cosmopolitan flair, from its colonial-era eateries to jazz bars that echo with old-time melodies. The graffiti here mirrors this eclectic legacy, transforming blank walls into corridors of memory and fantasy. Murals depict Kolkata’s colonial facades, vintage street scenes, and the city’s literary giants, as if capturing snapshots from a sepia-tinted past. Amidst the modern murals, one might find tributes to Satyajit Ray, lines from Rabindranath Tagore, or vibrant portrayals of neon nightlife. Historically, Park Street was also a gathering place for intellectuals and artists during the post-independence period, and today’s graffiti channels that creative energy, blending it with a contemporary, youthful edge. Strolling past the murals feels like flipping through a living scrapbook of Kolkata’s layered history.
